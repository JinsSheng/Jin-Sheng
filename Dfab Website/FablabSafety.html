Safety First!

General Safety Rules & Regulations

- Students are not allowed to enter and work in any laboratory or workshop in the
absence of the lecturer-in-charge or Technical Supporting Officer (TSO).

- Attire: Collared shirt or T-shirt and full-length trousers, Covered shoes, long
hair must be tied up, use of additional safety equipment if required.

- Be familiar with the location of the first-aid box, safety eye-wash and /or shower,
chemical spill kit within each laboratory and workshop.

- Know the location of all fire escape exits and assembly area.

- Do not tamper with all safety devices and machine guards

- Seek help from your lecturer-in-charge or TSO whenever you are in doubt.

- Report any UNSAFE conditions or UNSAFE acts to your lecturer-in-charge or TSO
