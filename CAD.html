We were introduced to fusion 360 and the first project we did is a 3D model
of a chess piece.

A custom name tag that we did on fusion 360 right after, we 3D printed our
models out.

We were tasked to create an object that is nearly impossible to fabricate
manually.

To test the 3D printer capabilities, we form to groups of 4 and modeled a
benchmark to 3D print. My part was to test the overhang of the printer.

We learnt about Parametric modelling in Fusion 360 it a method where you
use parameters to set the dimensions of part or all of your model. The cool
part is that after your model is complete, and given that everything is in
order, you can go back and change the parameters.
